Brewing

Making a great cup of coffee at home is simple. Choose your equipment, and follow the steps below.

Aeropress

Ideal for making a single cup of coffee.

  1. Ideal for making a single cup of coffee.
  2. Bring water to a boil and set it aside for 30 seconds.
  3. Finely grind your coffee.
  4. Briefly rinse a paper filter to ensure no papery taste.
  5. Place the rinsed filter in the cap and twist into the chamber.
  6. Add ⅛ cup of ground coffee to the chamber.
  7. Set your timer for 2 minutes, and add ½ cup hot water.
  8. Stir a few times.
  9. Add another ¼ cup of water and give it a few more stirs.
  10. Use the plunger to create a vacuum seal and let it brew for two minutes.
  11. At the two minute mark, begin to press the coffee into your cup (this should take around 20 seconds).
  12. Add ⅓ cup of water to the brewed concentrate.
  13. Enjoy your coffee.

Chemex

Perfect for brewing up to eight 5-oz cups of coffee.

  1. Bring water to a boil and set it aside for 30 seconds.
  2. Wet the Chemex filter and place it in the Chemex vessel.
  3. Place ¼ cup of fine, drip-grind coffee in the filter.
  4. Pour 4 cups of water over grounds slowly in a circular motion over 3.5 minutes.
