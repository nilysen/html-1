2014 Trio | El Dorado County

Our best blended wine ever from three El Dorado grown Italian varietals featuring Primitivo, Dolcetto, and Sangiovese. A huge wine showing masses of ripe cherry and black plum followed by intense blackberry on the nose that follows through nicely on the well structured mouthfeel.

2014 Chardonnay | Sonoma Coast

The fruit of the Chardonnay grape literally explodes from the glass.
